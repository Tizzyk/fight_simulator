How To

Get Started

  • Start by selecting a gender. Fights can only be between the same gender, but fighters may challenge opponents from different promotions and weight classes. Fighters cannot fight each other.
  • Once you select a gender all the available fighters will appear in the fighter list. You may filter the fighter list further by selection a promotion. Choosing a promotion will load the weight classes available in that organization.
  • When you have a fighter selected in each corner, click the "FIGHT!" button to start a simulation. Each corner has a reset button if you want to clear your selection before the fight.
  • The results panel for the fight will appear at the bottom of the page.
  • After your fight has been simulated, use the 'Start Over' button to simulate another matchup.

User History

  • To save your fights, register for an account and login. Your fights will be automatically saved as long as you are logged in.
  • If you would like to clear your history, click the 'Clear History' button at the bottom of the page. This will only clear your visible history. Every simulation is recorded and adjusts the fighters win/loss records accordingly, but only for your account.
