How It Works

Where to Start

  1. Start by selecting a gender. Fights can only be between the same gender, but fighters may challenge opponents from different promotions and weight classes.
  2. Once you select a gender all the available fighters will appear in the fighter list. You may filter the fighter list further by selection a promotion. Choosing a promotion will load the weight classes available in that organization.
  3. When you have a fighter selected in each corner, click the "FIGHT!" button to start a simulation.
  4. The results of the fight simulation will appear in the result panel at the bottom of the page.
  5. To reset the menus at any time or after a simulation, click the "Start Over" button below the results panel.

MMA Basic Info

Weight Class Definitions

The weights listed are the maximum a fighter can weigh:

  • Womens Strawweight - 115 lbs.
  • Womens Bantamweight - 135 lbs.
  • Mens Flyweight - 125 lbs.
  • Mens Bantamweight - 135 lbs.
  • Mens Featherweight - 145 lbs.
  • Mens Lightweight - 155 lbs.
  • Mens Welterweight - 170 lbs.
  • Mens Middleweight - 185 lbs.
  • Mens Light Heavyweight - 205 lbs.
  • Mens Heavyweight - 265 lbs.
